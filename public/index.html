What Is Our Tool?

We are an AI-powered YouTube summarizer tool for gamers to discover optimal competitive strategies.

In competitive gaming, identifying optimal strategies is a key determinant of success. However, the abundance of video content available on platforms like YouTube makes it difficult for gamers to extract actionable insights efficiently.

This project introduces an AI-powered YouTube summarizer tailored specifically for gamers, leveraging OpenAI and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology.
